About Us

We are final year students of Nepal Engineering College, passionate about revolutionizing agriculture in Nepal. Our project, the Oyster Mushroom Cultivation and Disease Management Platform, is a cutting-edge online initiative designed to support oyster mushroom farmers in Nepal.

This comprehensive platform offers a wide range of resources, including step-by-step cultivation guidance, handy tools, and invaluable disease information. By connecting farmers with experts, the platform enables paid appointments, expert advice, and access to vital subsidy details.

Utilizing Convolutional Neural Networks, our platform's disease detection system significantly improves mushroom cultivation practices, promotes collaboration among stakeholders, and strategically incorporates technology to revolutionize Nepal's agricultural sector.

Moreover, the platform acts as a channel for governmental organizations to share important notices, gather valuable insights, and contribute to the thriving agricultural community.

This project also serves as a versatile prototype for similar disease detection endeavors in agriculture, offering a robust foundation for innovation and advancement in agricultural technology.
